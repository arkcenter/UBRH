Essential PC Building Tips

  • Ensure CPU and motherboard socket compatibility (e.g., LGA 1200 CPU needs LGA 1200 motherboard)
  • AMD AM4 CPUs work with AM4 motherboards, AM5 with AM5 motherboards
  • Always choose at least a Gold-rated power supply for reliability
  • For ITX cases, make sure to buy an ITX motherboard
  • Match monitor refresh rate with GPU capability (don't pair 60Hz monitor with high-end GPU)
  • Check fan direction: typically 3 intake in front, 1 exhaust in back
  • Build on a non-carpeted surface and avoid wearing socks (static electricity)
⚠️ Important Warning: Do not trust bottleneck calculators - they are inaccurate 95% of the time!
